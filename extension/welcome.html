Before you start

One-time setup

Two steps, once. After this it just works in the background.

⚙ Connect the extension to your vault

  1. Install the desktop app and register this extension's native host: python install.py --extension-id <your-extension-id>, then restart your browser.
  2. Open the Maliketh desktop app and create your vault with a strong master password.
